# Sales and Performance Analyst of a Company With SQL
## Project Background
Apex Velo Solutions, established in 2015, is a specialized retailer that supplies high-performance cycling equipment and engineering components to enthusiasts worldwide via its dedicated e-commerce platform and service hubs.
The company maintains a comprehensive inventory of complete bicycle systems, precision maintenance parts, and technical apparel that caters to both professional athletes and recreational riders. This portfolio is meticulously curated to provide the necessary tools and gear that optimize riding performance, safety, and mechanical reliability across various terrains.

Insights and recommondations are provided on the following key areas:
Demographic and gender analysis: Finding countries that buying our products and the ages of customers uesd to buy.
Regional comparsion: finding the most solds products in countries and lowest, and how can we make the sales better.
Performance Analysis: Analyzing the performance of products depending on sales.
